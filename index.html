"There are eighty-eight keys on a piano and within that, an entire universe."

Nicolas creates piano music to get lost in your dreams, to meditate and to let yourself go.
His love has been influenced by his Mama, who has been able to arouse enthusiasm for this particular instrument.

  • “Without a piano I don’t know how to stand, don’t know what to do with my hands.”

    Nicolas’s journey is accompanied by his highly creative Mama, who together with him try to capture all the life stages.

  • “The important thing is to feel your music, really feel it and believe it.”

    And this is during this very journey that new stories arise, stories that will turn into new pieces.

  • "Music gives a soul to the universe, wings to the mind, flight to the imagination and life to everything."

    It sounds like a musical frame for memories. In other words: the soundtrack for the own life.

  • "The piano as a medium for expression is a whole world by itself. No other instrument can replace its own emotion"

    In this domestic atmosphere, every stage becomes the frame for memories. Personal stories and dreaming images create a unique experience.
